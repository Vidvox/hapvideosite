What Workflows Are The Hap Video Codecs Commonly Used In?

Live Event and Concert Production

Hap is used to power the some of the biggest live events and concerts.

Set Design and Projection Mapping

Hap is the perfect codec choice for playing back video to large numbers of displays and projectors.

Interactive Digital Signage and Installations

The Hap video codecs are a great choice for file assets used for real-time responsive projects where efficient movie playback is needed.

360 Video and VR

Hap makes it possible to composite ultra high resolution 360 and other warped video layers with alpha channels in gaming and VR environments.

Media Servers

The Hap video codecs are supported on most of the major media servers.

Creative Coding

Hap is a favorite for creating custom applications and workflows using popular creative coding toolsets.
